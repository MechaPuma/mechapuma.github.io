"CREATIVE" CONTENT

"Creative" Content is quite the series here on MechaPuma. In this series, I make fun of various fandoms, such as the furry fandom, brony fandom, Sonic fandom, Cuphead fandom, Flat Earth Society, and more! Each episode is a new roast on a community! This series isn't to be taken too seriously though, as I do not actually hate on these communities. Although sometimes harsh, it should be noted that it's for the entertainment of the YouTube community and should be funny, not hurtful! It accounts for a good 75,000 overall channel views thanks to you guys and girls!

DONNIE (DONALD TRUMP)

For me, this is the most entertaining series to produce—but what are these Donnie songs about? Throughout the years, I've seen countless amounts of music video "parodies" online, yet none of them seem to be all that funny... or punny for that matter. I've seen artists such as Filthy Frank (Papa Franku) and Dave Days create music videos on popular figures—especially relevant politicians. Since I am personally facepalming at the current state of American politics, I decided to express that annoyance and outrage in a music video in order to channel my inner libtard! Hopefully you all enjoy the Trump parodies—because more are coming soon!
